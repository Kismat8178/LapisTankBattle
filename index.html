How to Play

Player 1 (Keyboard):

  WASD: Move Tank

  Q/E: Rotate Turret

  Spacebar: Shoot


Player 2 (Keyboard):

  Arrow Keys: Move Tank

  Comma (,)/Period (.): Rotate Turret

  Enter: Shoot


Mobile Controls (VS CPU):

  Player 1: Joystick Bottom-Left (Move & Aim), Fire Button Bottom-Right


Mobile Controls (2 Player Local):

  Player 1: Joystick Top-Left, Fire Button Top-Right

  Player 2: Joystick Bottom-Right, Fire Button Bottom-Left
